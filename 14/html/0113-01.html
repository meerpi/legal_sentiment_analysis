Mr. Justice Scott.

Held that where no question of law is presented, nor any exception, taken to the opinion of the court; and the finding of the court or verdict of the jury is not without evidence to support it, this court will not disturb the judgment of the court below. State Bank vs. Conway, 13 Ark.
